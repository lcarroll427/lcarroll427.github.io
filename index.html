About Me

Hello! My name is Leanna Carroll. I am currently pursuing my MLIS degree at the University of Denver. I am also a self-published author of two novels.

Books

Last Diamond Rose
My First Novel

Investigating a ten-year-old murder case upon the discovery of new evidence, Montgomery detective Isla Banks finds herself face-to-face with the convicted killer, and discovers he is not all he seems. Was this case closed too soon? And what of the adopted daughter, who disappeared the same night of the crime? Where is Diamond McMullen, and why did she run?

Between the Tracks
My Second Novel

This coming-of-age story explores the inner workings of the mind in order to reveal the other side of a bad influence, and how someone's actions can go from inconvenient to dangerous in a matter of seconds, even and especially inwards. There is always more than meets the eye.
